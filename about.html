Who we are

A supplier that acts like a partner

We started MAGL in Durban with a simple observation: contractors don't lose money on materials — they lose it waiting for materials. So we built a supply business around the delivery, not just the product.

Today we supply bricks, cement, steel and timber to residential, commercial and civil projects in every province of South Africa, and we're preparing to launch our own logistics division to close the loop.
